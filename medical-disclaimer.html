1. Purpose of NurturedPet

NurturedPet is designed to support pet caregivers in managing their pet's medication schedules, health tracking, and wellness routines. The App provides:

  • Medication reminders and scheduling tools
  • Health monitoring features (such as respiratory rate tracking)
  • Wellness assessments and logging
  • AI-powered informational guidance
  • Record-keeping and sharing capabilities

NurturedPet does not provide:

3. AI Care Companion Limitations

NurturedPet includes an AI-powered Care Companion feature. It's important to understand its limitations:

What the AI Can Do

  • Provide general educational information about pet care
  • Offer emotional support and encouragement
  • Answer general questions about medications (based on publicly available information)
  • Help you track patterns and observations

What the AI Cannot Do

  • Diagnose your pet's health condition
  • Recommend specific treatments or medications

5. When to Contact Your Veterinarian

Always contact your veterinarian if your pet:

  • Shows signs of illness, pain, or distress
  • Experiences changes in appetite, energy, or behavior
  • Has a bad reaction to medication
  • Misses multiple doses of medication
  • Shows breathing difficulties or changes in respiratory rate
  • Has any condition that concerns you
