Frequently Asked Questions

Is NurturedPet a replacement for veterinary care?

No. NurturedPet is a caregiving tool to help you track medications, monitor health patterns, and stay organized. Always follow your veterinarian's guidance for medical decisions. Our AI Care Companion provides supportive information, not medical advice.

Is my pet's data private?

Absolutely. NurturedPet uses a local-first architecture ‚Äî your data stays on your device. We don't sell your information, and there's no account required. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Email us at support@nurturedpet.com with "Bug Report" or "Feature Request" in the subject line. Screenshots or screen recordings are incredibly helpful. Beta testers can also submit feedback with screenshots directly through TestFlight ‚Äî just tap the arrow icon to send a screenshot and notes straight to our support team.

Can I use NurturedPet for multiple pets?

Yes! NurturedPet supports multiple pet profiles, each with their own medications, health records, and care schedules. There's no limit on the number of pets you can add.

How do I join the beta?

Visit nurturedpet.app to join our waitlist. Beta access is distributed through Apple TestFlight, and we'll send you an invitation when a spot opens up.

What devices are supported?

NurturedPet requires an iPhone running iOS 17 or later. iPad support and additional platforms are planned for the future.
