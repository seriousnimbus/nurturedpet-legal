üíæ 4. How We Store Your Data

4.1 Local-First Architecture

NurturedPet is designed with privacy at its core:

  • Primary Storage: All your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework
  • No Account Required: You can use NurturedPet without creating an account or providing personal identifying information
  • You Control Your Data: Your pet care data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to sync or share it

4.2 Optional iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync:

  • Your data is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account
  • We cannot access your iCloud data ‚Äî it belongs to you
  • Sync can be disabled at any time in Settings
  • Disabling sync does not delete your local data

4.3 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Local data is protected by iOS device encryption
  • All network communications use TLS/HTTPS encryption
  • API keys and sensitive credentials are securely managed
  • We do not store payment information (handled by Apple)
