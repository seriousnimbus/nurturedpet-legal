What is Care Reach?

Care Reach is NurturedPet's optional SMS and voice call feature that helps you stay on top of your pet's medication schedule. When enabled, you may receive:

  • Medication reminders when doses are due or overdue
  • Wellness check-in messages
  • Milestone celebrations (e.g., dose streaks, care anniversaries)
  • Voice phone calls for critical medication reminders

Care Reach is a premium feature of the NurturedPet iOS app. Messages are transactional and directly related to your pet's care — we never send marketing or promotional messages.

How You Opt In

SMS and voice communications are opt-in only. You will never receive messages unless you explicitly enable Care Reach within the NurturedPet app.

Opt-In Process

  1. Open the NurturedPet app on your iPhone
  2. Navigate to Settings → Notifications → Care Reach
  3. Select your preferred notification intensity level (which includes Care Reach)
  4. Enter and verify your mobile phone number
  5. Confirm your consent to receive SMS and/or voice calls

By completing this process, you consent to receive transactional SMS messages and voice calls from NurturedPet at the phone number you provide. You may revoke this consent at any time.

How to Opt Out

You can stop receiving messages at any time using either method:

Reply STOP: Text STOP to +1 (855) 924-4340 at any time. You will receive a confirmation message and no further SMS messages will be sent.

In-App: Open NurturedPet → Settings → Notifications → disable Care Reach. This immediately stops all SMS and voice communications.

To resume messages after opting out, text START to +1 (855) 924-4340 or re-enable Care Reach in the app.

For help, text HELP to +1 (855) 924-4340 or email support@nurturedpet.com.

Privacy & Data Handling

  • Your phone number is stored locally on your device and transmitted to our server only when sending a message
  • Phone numbers are never sold, shared with third parties, or used for marketing
  • SMS delivery is powered by Twilio, which processes your phone number solely for message delivery
  • Message logs are retained for up to 30 days for delivery verification, then deleted
  • All communications are encrypted in transit via TLS/HTTPS
